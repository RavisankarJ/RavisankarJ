Numerate Fish

Catch the missing numbers to solve the given equation.

The goal of this game is the immune warrior (player) has to survive from pathogens in the stipulated time.

How to fill the numbers?

  • Click / Touch on the bubble which has the required number.
  • The fish will catch the number
  • Note: If fish touch or cross wrong number it will affect fish's health.

Fish Health

  • You can view the fish's health on top left corner
  • Warrior's health will decrease if fish catches wrong bubbles
  • Fish's health will increase if you catch special right bubble with food.

Level, Score and Attempts

  • You win if you give right answers all the attempts.
  • Each level will increase the difficulty
  • The more you answer the more you score.
