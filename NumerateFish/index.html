Numerate Fish

Catch the missing numbers to solve the given equation.

The question is open ended. So there will be many answers for a question. The player has to wait for the answer bubble to appear and then catch it.

Question / Equation

  • The question or equation will be displayed on the top right side.
  • An arrow will be shown each time the question changes.
  • The questions are open ended. So there will be many answers for a same question.

How to answer the question?

  • Click / Touch on the bubble which has the required number.
  • The fish will catch the number
  • For 2 operand questions you need to catch 2 bubbles
  • For 3 operand questions you need to catch 3 bubbles
  • The numbers you caught will be filled in the question / equation on top right side.
  • For more than one operand questions, incase if you caught a wrong bubble and you may click on the fish to release the bublle.
  • Note: If fish touch or cross wrong number it will affect fish's health.

Fish Health

  • You can view the fish's health on top left corner
  • Fish's health will decrease if fish catches wrong bubbles
  • Fish's health will increase if you catch special right bubble with food.

Level, Score and Attempts

  • You win if you give right answers all the attempts. The number of attempts will be displayed on top left side.
  • The current level will be displayed on top center. Each level will increase the difficulty.
  • The more you answer the more you collect coins. You can view your coins near the coin collection picture.

Home, Info, and Music

  • During play time if you want to quit you may click the home button.
  • Anytime if you want to study this instructions click the info button.
  • If you want to mute the music click the music button to mute and unmute.
