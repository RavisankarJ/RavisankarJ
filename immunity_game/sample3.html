Immune Warrior

Surrvive against the pathogens

Warrior Moves

  • : 'Left Arrow' or Swipe left to move left
  • : 'Right Arrow' or Swipe right to move right
  • : 'Up Arrow' or Swipe up to jump

Warrior Health

  • You can view the warrior's health on top left corner
  • Warrior's health will decrease if pathogens escape from you
  • Warrior's health will decide its size and jumping capapcity

Time, Level and Score

  • You win if you survive upto the time specified in top left.
  • Each level will increase the difficulty
  • The more you kill pathogens the more you score.

Warrior Powers

  • Vitmain C will increase warrior's size. : C
  • Vitmain E will create explosion and kills pathogens. : E
  • Vitamin B6 will create a wall which stops pathogens to escape. : B
  • Zinc will increase warrior's health. : Z

Healthy Foods and unhealthy Foods

  • Healthy foods like fruits, vegetables, nuts, milk and egg give powers as Vitamin C, E, B6 and Zinc.
  • Unhealthy foods like oil foods (chips, finger chips), frozen foods (pizza, donut) and cool drinks decrease warrior's health.
